Brownie

Description

Brownie is a classic recipe from USA. It's like a cake that doens't grow, but still very delicious!

Ingredients

  • 170 grams of unsalted butter
  • 120 grams of 70% dark chocolate
  • 200 grams of refined sugar
  • 3 eggs
  • a spoonful vanilla paste
  • 40 grams of bitter cocoa powder
  • 60 grams of wheat flour
  • a spoonful of salt
  • 170 gram of chocolate chips
  • a spoonful of instant coffee
  • a spoonful of sea salt
  • 100 gram of roasted nuts

Steps

  1. Melt the butter in the pan, remove from the heat as soon as it turns golden and place in the bowl
  2. Add the coffee and dark chocolate to the butter. Stir until melted. Set aside.
  3. Beat the eggs with sugar until completely dissolved (+- 8 minutes)
  4. Mix the melting chocolate with the eggs
  5. Add the vanilla paste
  6. Chop the walnuts and add the chocolate chips and sea salt
  7. Put in a 22x22 square pan
  8. 20-24 min in the oven at 180Â°C
  9. Leave to cool for at least 2 hours before cutting
