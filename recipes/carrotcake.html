Carrot Cake

Description

Carrot cake is a popular dessert in Brazil that is loved by everybody. It's simple and easy to make, also very delicious! This cake is a simple and practical option for every moment, this recipe is made in a blender adn can be ready in less than an hour.

Ingredients

Batter

  • 3 medium carrots
  • 3 eggs
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 1 cup canola oil
  • 2 cups wheat flour
  • Salt
  • Baking powder

Frosting

  • 5 tablespoons sugar
  • 3 tablespoons 50% Cocoa Powder
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons milk

Steps

Batter

  1. In a blender, combine 3 medium carrots, 3 eggs, 1 cup of canola oil, and 2 cups of sugar. Blend until smooth.
  2. In a bowl, combine 2 cups of wheat flour, 1 pinch of salt, and 1 tablespoon of baking powder and mix.
  3. Then, add the mixture from the blender to the bowl.
  4. Using a whisk, mix until smooth.
  5. Transfer the batter to a greased and floured pan.
  6. Bake in a preheated oven at 180 degrees Celsius (356 degrees Fahrenheit) for 40 minutes.

Frosting

  1. In a saucepan, combine 5 tablespoons of sugar, 3 tablespoons of 50% cocoa powder, 2 tablespoons of milk, and 2 tablespoons of unsalted butter.
  2. Stir and cook until boiling.
  3. Once the mixture pulls away from the bottom of the pan, the frosting is ready.
  4. Wait for the cake to cool and pour the hot frosting over it.
  5. Spread the frosting over the cake and let it cool before serving.
